About us

Where discipline meets stage energy

Blue Heart Dance Academy helps students discover rhythm, fitness, confidence, and performance presence through structured dance training in Atrauli, Aligarh.

Led by Tarun Singh, Director & Choreographer, the academy blends professional choreography with a welcoming local studio culture for beginners, kids, performers, and wedding groups.
